Mother Bonding

The bond between a mom and son is a special one. So is that between mother and daughter, but we are all about celebrating our boys today—on Son's Day!. As mothers, we teach our sons how to be kind, gentle, and respectful. We want to make sure they know it’s just as okay to feel upset and shed tears as it is to be angry and mad.

We searched high and low for a collection of parenting quotes that specifically celebrate the special bond between a mom and her son. These quotes about mothers and sons include adages from famous writers, old proverbs that have stood the test of time, sentimental statements shared by famous men about their own moms, and words of wisdom from the tribe of #boymoms. Read on and think about how lucky you are to have that special bond between a mother and her son.
